## Overview

Nimbus Dashboards provides a live view of your operations by connecting directly to your most important data streams. Create beautiful, TV-ready displays that keep your entire team aligned on daily goals and performance indicators.

Set up custom threshold alerts to notify your team via email or chat the moment a metric drops below or spikes above your target. Stay proactive and resolve operational bottlenecks before they impact your business.
