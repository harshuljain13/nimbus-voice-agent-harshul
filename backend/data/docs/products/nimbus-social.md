# Nimbus Social

*Manage, monitor, and measure your social presence.*

**Category:** Marketing

Nimbus Social is an all-in-one social media management tool that lets you schedule posts, listen to brand conversations, and analyze performance across networks. Streamline your social strategy from a single intuitive dashboard.

## Overview

Nimbus Social simplifies the way businesses handle their social media presence across multiple platforms. With our unified content calendar, your team can draft, review, and schedule posts weeks in advance. Collaborative approval workflows ensure that every piece of content meets your brand guidelines before it goes live, saving time and preventing costly mistakes.
The platform also features powerful social listening and analytics tools. Track brand mentions, monitor industry keywords, and gauge customer sentiment in real time. Our comprehensive reporting dashboards break down follower growth, engagement rates, and click-through metrics, helping you understand exactly what content resonates with your audience and how to improve your overall social strategy.

## Key features
- Unified social media publishing calendar
