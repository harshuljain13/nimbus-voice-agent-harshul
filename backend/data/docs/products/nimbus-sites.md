# Nimbus Sites

*Build high-converting landing pages and websites instantly.*

**Category:** Marketing

Nimbus Sites is a visual website and landing page builder designed to help marketers capture more leads. Create beautiful, mobile-optimized pages with integrated forms and native A/B testing.

## Overview

Nimbus Sites gives marketing professionals the power to launch stunning web pages without needing a developer. The platform features a robust drag-and-drop interface and a vast library of conversion-optimized templates tailored for various industries. Whether you are launching a product, hosting a webinar, or capturing newsletter signups, you can design and publish your page in record time.
To help you maximize your marketing ROI, Nimbus Sites includes powerful conversion rate optimization tools. You can easily set up A/B tests to compare different headlines, images, or calls to action to see what drives the most conversions. Built-in lead capture forms automatically sync with your CRM and marketing tools, ensuring that no prospect falls through the cracks.
