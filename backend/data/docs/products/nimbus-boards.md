# Nimbus Boards

*Visualize workflows with intuitive kanban and online whiteboards.*

**Category:** Projects & Collaboration

Nimbus Boards provides flexible visual workspaces for brainstorming, sprint planning, and agile workflow management. Teams can seamlessly switch between structured kanban boards and freeform digital whiteboards.

## Overview

Bring your ideas to life and streamline your agile processes with Nimbus Boards. Whether your team is mapping out a new user journey on a limitless whiteboard or moving task cards through a structured kanban pipeline, this tool adapts to your creative and operational needs. Sticky notes, drawing tools, and flowchart shapes make remote brainstorming sessions feel just as interactive as being in the same room.

Beyond simple ideation, Nimbus Boards is built for action. You can easily convert whiteboard sticky notes into trackable kanban cards with assignees, due dates, and custom tags. Automated triggers help move cards between columns based on status updates, saving your team from manual data entry. It is the perfect bridge between unstructured creativity and organized execution.
