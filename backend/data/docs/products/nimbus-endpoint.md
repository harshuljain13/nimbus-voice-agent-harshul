# Nimbus Endpoint

*Comprehensive device management and endpoint security.*

**Category:** IT & Security

Nimbus Endpoint allows IT teams to manage, secure, and monitor company-owned and bring-your-own devices from a single cloud console. It protects your endpoints against malware and unauthorized access.

## Overview

Nimbus Endpoint simplifies mobile device management (MDM) and endpoint protection for modern, distributed workforces. IT administrators can easily deploy applications, enforce security policies, and wipe lost devices remotely, ensuring that corporate data remains secure regardless of where employees are working.

In addition to device management, Nimbus Endpoint includes next-generation antivirus and threat detection capabilities. It continuously monitors device health, blocks malicious software, and provides instant alerts for suspicious activities, giving your security team the tools they need to respond to threats in real time.

## Key features
- Cross-platform device management (Windows, Mac, iOS, Android)
- Remote lock and wipe capabilities
- Automated patch management and OS updates
- Next-generation antivirus (NGAV)
- Bring Your Own Device (BYOD) separation
- Application deployment and blacklisting
- USB device control and encryption enforcement
- Real-time device health monitoring
- Geolocation tracking for lost devices
- Automated compliance reporting
