# Nimbus Expense

*Streamline corporate spending and employee reimbursement workflows*

**Category:** Finance & Accounting

Control company spending with smart corporate cards and automated expense reporting. Nimbus Expense eliminates manual data entry and accelerates employee reimbursements.

## Overview

Nimbus Expense transforms how your company handles employee spending and out-of-pocket reimbursements. Employees can simply snap a picture of their receipts using our mobile app, and our optical character recognition technology instantly extracts the details to create an expense line item. Managers can approve reports on the go, speeding up the entire reimbursement cycle.

Take control of your budget by issuing physical and virtual Nimbus corporate cards with built-in spending limits. You can set custom policies to automatically flag out-of-policy expenses and route high-value requests to senior management. Real-time spend analytics give finance teams the visibility they need to optimize cash flow and enforce compliance across the organization.
