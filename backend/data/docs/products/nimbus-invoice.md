# Nimbus Invoice

*Effortless invoicing and fast online payment collection*

**Category:** Finance & Accounting

Create professional invoices, set up recurring billing, and accept online payments in minutes. Nimbus Invoice helps you get paid faster and improves your cash flow.

## Overview

Nimbus Invoice takes the friction out of billing your clients. You can easily design custom invoices that reflect your brand, send them via email or SMS, and track when they are opened. By integrating directly with major payment gateways, your clients can pay you instantly using credit cards, bank transfers, or digital wallets.

Managing recurring revenue is simple with our automated subscription billing engine. You can set up flexible billing schedules, handle proration, and send automated payment reminders for overdue accounts. The centralized dashboard gives you a clear view of outstanding balances and revenue trends, ensuring nothing slips through the cracks.
