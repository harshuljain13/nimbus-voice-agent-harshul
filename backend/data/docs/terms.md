# Terms & Policies

## Free trial

All Nimbus products come with a 14-day free trial that includes full access to premium features. No credit card is required to start your trial, ensuring a risk-free evaluation period. At the end of the trial, you can seamlessly upgrade to a paid plan without losing any of your setup data.

## Billing

We offer both monthly and annual billing cycles, with annual plans receiving a 20 percent discount. We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and wire transfers for enterprise accounts exceeding $5,000 USD. All transactions are processed in USD, though local currency display options are available in the billing dashboard.

## Cancellation

Administrators can cancel their subscription or downgrade their plan at any time directly from the billing portal. Upon cancellation, your account will remain active until the end of your current billing cycle. We retain your data for 60 days post-cancellation to allow for easy reactivation or data export before permanent deletion.

## Service level agreement (SLA)

We guarantee a 99.9 percent monthly uptime for all standard plans and 99.99 percent for enterprise tiers. If we fail to meet this SLA, affected customers are eligible for service credits applied to their next billing cycle. Credits range from 10 percent to 50 percent of the monthly fee depending on the severity of the outage.

## Security & compliance

Nimbus is fully compliant with SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, and global privacy frameworks including GDPR and CCPA. All customer data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3 and at rest using AES-256 encryption standards. We also conduct regular third-party penetration testing to ensure our infrastructure remains secure against emerging threats.

## Data residency

Customers can choose to host their data in our secure regional data centers located in the US, EU, IN, or AU. Data residency selections are made during account creation and ensure compliance with local data localization regulations.

## Support

Standard plans include email and live chat support during regular business hours. Enterprise customers receive priority phone support and a dedicated technical account manager. All users have unlimited access to the Nimbus Knowledge base and community forums.
